Differences from PM

  1. Pandoc (as well as others) refers to ordinary weak emphasis simply as emphasis. Ordinary weak emphasis is the term used throughout this documentation for the element in order to differentiate it from the general concept of emphasis, which encompasses all emphasis elements, i.e.

    1. ordinary weak emphasis,

    2. ordinary strong emphasis,

    3. special weak emphasis and

    4. special stong emphasis.
