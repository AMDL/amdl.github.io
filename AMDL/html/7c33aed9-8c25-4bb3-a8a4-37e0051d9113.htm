Which Sandcastle?

Sandcastle Help File Builder, a.k.a SHFB.

Which Markdown?

Pandoc's Markdown, a.k.a. PM, with extensions, restrictions, and interpretations.

Why AMDL?

AMDL is acronym for Assistance Markdown Language. It's the bastard child of MAML and MD.

Why not MAMDL?

  • It isn't created by Microsoft.

Why not AML?

  • That's the MAML file extension.

Why not AMD?

  • That one's taken as well.

Isn't AMDL also taken?

  • Advanced Micronic Devices Ltd. are hardly competition.

Wasn't it something I studied about in college?

  • Nope, you'd need LATEX for that.
